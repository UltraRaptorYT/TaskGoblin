# TaskGoblin OPC Hackathon Pitch Script

Good afternoon everyone.

Let me begin with a simple question.

Your team agreed to do it. So why did nobody do it?

Important work is assigned in Telegram every day, but Telegram is designed for conversation, not accountability.

Someone mentions a deadline. Another person vaguely agrees to handle it. Then someone sends a GIF, the conversation moves on, and the commitment disappears.

This is especially painful for a one-person company.

Every customer, freelancer, and deadline competes for the same founder's attention. A solo founder cannot keep adding headcount whenever coordination becomes more complicated.

That is why I built TaskGoblin.

TaskGoblin is an AI project-operations agent that turns work buried inside Telegram chats into an accountable project board.

It gives one founder the project-management capacity that would traditionally require another person.

The problem is not the lack of another task manager. It is that a solo founder does not have time to translate every conversation into one.

TaskGoblin works through four stages.

First, it observes by reading an imported Telegram conversation or project document.

Second, it identifies tasks, owners, deadlines, decisions, blockers, and risks.

Third, it organises them into an editable project state with confidence and source evidence.

Finally, it acts by identifying accountability gaps and generating the appropriate follow-up.

Let me show you what that looks like.

## Live demonstration

**[Open the Telegram demonstration.]**

This is a realistic project conversation.

Useful updates are mixed with jokes, memes, vague promises, and a “this is fine” GIF. A Scrum report has been seen by everyone but claimed by nobody.

A person can reconstruct the plan by reading everything, but doing that repeatedly turns the founder into the bottleneck.

**[Export and import the conversation into TaskGoblin.]**

TaskGoblin normalises the Telegram messages and media records, then asks the AI to produce a structured project scan.

**[Show the completed project board.]**

Instead of returning another paragraph, TaskGoblin creates an editable project state.

We can immediately see what must happen, who is responsible, what is blocked, and what information is missing.

When I open a task, I can see its owner, deadline, status, confidence, and source message. Human judgment remains in control because the user can verify and correct the AI.

**[Select an unassigned task.]**

Here, TaskGoblin has identified a ghost task with no clear owner. I can assign it, update its status, and generate a professional, friendly, or playful Goblin follow-up.

TaskGoblin can already send reminders through Telegram. Next, I am adding per-project linking, scheduled delivery, and continuous processing of new messages.

## Why this is more than summarisation

This is different from asking a general chatbot to summarise a conversation.

A summary is disposable. TaskGoblin creates persistent project state using structured outputs, confidence, source evidence, and empty fields when facts are unknown instead of inventing them.

A summariser tells you what people discussed.

TaskGoblin gives a solo founder an operations system that determines what must happen next, preserves the evidence, and follows up before the work is forgotten.

## Market and business

I am beginning with one-person companies, solo agencies, and independent founders managing clients and collaborators through Telegram. TaskGoblin lets their project capacity grow without adding project-management headcount. It can later expand to other chat-native teams.

The business model is freemium SaaS. Pro adds higher AI limits, history, and reminder automation. Team plans add shared workspaces and controls. I will validate pricing through pilots based on time saved and on-time delivery.

## Why me and what is next

TaskGoblin is also an example of the company model it supports.

I built it as a solo founder, covering product, full-stack engineering, the AI pipeline, Telegram integration, and deployment. AI-assisted development and managed infrastructure let one person cover roles that previously required a team.

The MVP supports Telegram and document imports, structured AI scans, source traceability, an editable board, dynamic project health, persistence, and Telegram reminders.

My next steps are completing project-level Telegram linking, adding scheduled automation, and running real pilots.

I am looking for solo founders and small agencies to test TaskGoblin on real projects, together with product and AI mentorship.

I want to prove that one founder, supported by an AI operations agent, can manage more projects without adding project-management headcount.

A one-person company should scale revenue without scaling administrative work.

TaskGoblin gives one founder the operational capacity of a team.

Thank you.
